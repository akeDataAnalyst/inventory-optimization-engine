1) Inventory Control and Reorder Intelligence
Title: Inventory Health, ABC Classification, and Reorder Point Planner.
Description: Build a simulated inventory dataset for pumps, solar parts, generators, and water-treatment items, then analyze stock health, fast/slow movers, ABC categories, safety stock, reorder points, and stockout risk. This directly reflects stores and inventory management, which is one of the main responsibilities in the JD.

Problem: The company can lose sales, delay projects, or increase emergency purchasing when stock levels are not monitored well, especially for critical imported and local items. Lead time changes and demand variability make it hard to know when to reorder.

Solution: Use Python/Jupyter Notebook to clean the simulated data and calculate KPIs such as inventory turnover, days of cover, ABC class, safety stock, and reorder point. Then build a Streamlit app that lets users filter by product family, warehouse, and supplier, while Google Sheets holds a simple replenishment tracker for managers who prefer Excel-style workflows.

Recommendation: Present the output as a decision tool, not just a chart project. In interviews, explain how it helps avoid stockouts, reduces overstock, and supports better purchase timing.

Stack to use: Python, Pandas, NumPy, Jupyter Notebook, Streamlit, Google Sheets, Plotly.
